Top 10 Sustainable Destinations in India

Sikkim

India's first fully organic state, promoting eco-friendly agriculture and tourism.

Kerala

Eco-tourism initiatives focus on preserving natural beauty and promoting responsible travel.

Meghalaya

Renowned for its living root bridges and sustainable farming practices.

Coorg

Model for sustainable agriculture and eco-friendly tourism.

Ladakh

Promotes responsible tourism to protect its fragile environment.

Rishikesh

Hub for eco-tourism and wellness, offering sustainable practices in various areas.

Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Focuses on eco-friendly tourism to protect unique marine ecosystems.

Spiti Valley

Community-led conservation efforts in a pristine environment.

Mawlynnong

Known as the "cleanest village in Asia," promoting sustainable living practices.

Khonoma

India's first green village, focused on sustainable agriculture and conservation.
